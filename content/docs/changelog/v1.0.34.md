# v1.0.34 — Schedule matrix image

_Released 2026-07-14_

## What's New
- **Post your league's schedule as an image — right from the Activity.** Open the schedule builder, apply your regular season, and a new **Post schedule to announcements** button appears. Ball Boy renders a clean matrix of every managed team's season and drops it in your announcements channel.
- **One row per manager, one column per week.** Each cell shows the opponent's logo with a home/away tag, byes included, all the way through the last week before your conference-championship game.
- **Rivalry games stand out.** Manager-vs-manager matchups are highlighted in gold, with per-team Home / Away / User-game tallies down the side.
- **Your league's colors.** The image uses your configured board color, and pings your announce role when one is set.
