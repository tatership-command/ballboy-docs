# v1.0.21 — /stream — Voice Channel Streams Stay Clean

_Released 2026-07-07_

## What's New

- **Streaming in a Discord voice channel now keeps the announcement tidy.** If you pick a `voice_channel` and don't choose a specific `profile`, `/stream` no longer tacks on all your saved Twitch/YouTube links — it just points people to the voice channel. Pick a `profile` (or pass a `url`) and that still shows alongside the voice channel.
