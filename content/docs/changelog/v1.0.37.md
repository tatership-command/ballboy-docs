# v1.0.37 — Custom Team Logos, Team Management & Smoother Sign-in

_Released 2026-07-17_

## What's New

**🖼️ Custom team logos everywhere**
Custom teams now show their logo across Discord — the roster board, standings, schedule, game threads, and the weekly matchup announcement, plus the "Team Claimed!" card. Add a team with a logo and it shows up just like a built-in team.

**🗂️ Manage your custom teams**
A new **Manage** tab in the Custom Teams screen lists every custom team you've added. Edit any field in place — name, abbreviation, nickname, conference, colors, or logo — with ownership and records kept intact. Or remove one with a confirm: it's cleanly deleted everywhere (roster, logo, role), and whoever owned it is released back to CPU.

**↩️ Return to the menu from anywhere**
Every Activity screen now has a **Return to Hub** button, so you're never stuck having to close and relaunch to get back.

**⚡ Smoother sign-in**
Opening the Activity now shows a quick "Signing you in…" indicator while it connects, instead of a Connect button that looked like you had to click it.

## Fixes

- Custom-team look-ups now pull in real team **colors** and **logos** reliably — a couple of data quirks in the College Football Data / ESPN sources were keeping them from loading.
