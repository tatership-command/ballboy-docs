# v1.0.19 — /stream — Announce Any Stream

_Released 2026-07-07_

## What's New

**New `/stream` command** — announce that you're going live for any game, not just scheduled season matchups.

- Perfect for **friendly and unscheduled games** — no game thread required. Any league member can run it.
- Name and ping your **opponent**, add a free-text **matchup or note** (e.g. "Texas vs Oklahoma — friendly"), and drop a **stream URL** (or let Ball Boy pull it from your saved profile links automatically).
- Choose who gets pinged: **@everyone**, your league's **stream role**, or a **specific role** of your choice.
- Ball Boy posts a 🔴 going-live announcement to your **streams channel** (or the current channel if you haven't set one) and confirms it to you privately.
