# v1.0.35 — Custom teams & conference realignment

_Released 2026-07-15_

## What's New
- **Add your own teams to a league.** Commissioners can now add a custom or missing team from the Ball Boy Activity — look it up from the College Football Data database with a live, editable preview, or enter it by hand (name, nickname, logo, colors). New teams are stored per-league, become claimable right away, and can never override a real seeded team.
- **Move teams between conferences.** Realign your league however you like. **`/conference assign`** moves one team to a new conference; **`/conference manage`** opens a picker to move several at once, one source conference at a time.
- **Realign any time — it sticks.** Conference assignments are saved per-league, apply to your current season immediately, and carry into every future season, so you can realign before or after a season is created.

## Fixes
- After a conference move, run **`/admin roles sync_all`** to move affected owners to their new conference role (the reply reminds you). Standings and the roster board update on their own.
