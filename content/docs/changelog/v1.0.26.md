# v1.0.26 — Claim Your Team in the App

_Released 2026-07-09_

## What's New

- **`/team claim` now opens the claim window right inside Discord.** Instead of claiming from the command line, `/team claim` launches the Ball Boy Activity — pick your league and team, link your game and stream accounts, and confirm, all in one guided in-app flow. Already own a team and just want to link your accounts? Use `/team connect`.
