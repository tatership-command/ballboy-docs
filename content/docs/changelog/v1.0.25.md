# v1.0.25 — Draft Mode Improvements

_Released 2026-07-09_

## What's New

- **Your claimed team is safe during a draft.** The draft will never hand a team you already own to another manager, and if your pick gets auto-selected (timer, auto-pick, or a skip) you keep the team you already claimed instead of being given a different one.
- **New "Set to auto" skip option.** In `/draft setup`, a skipped drafter — whether the clock runs out or a commissioner skips them — can now be auto-picked a team for their slot instead of being moved down the order.
- **Open teams into the draft from setup.** `/draft setup` now has a "release these owners' teams" picker — choose departing managers and their teams are freed into the draft pool when the draft starts.

## Fixes

- **The draft clock no longer stalls on an auto-pick** when the remaining options included a team another manager already owned.
