# v1.0.15 — Draft Mode

_Released 2026-07-06_

## What's New

**Draft Mode** — run a full team-selection draft right inside Discord.

- **Commissioner setup**: run `/draft setup` to configure the participant list, pick timer (from 60 seconds to 1 hour, or untimed), what happens on timeout (autopick, skip, or pause), the skip rule (back of line, swap with next, or remove), and personal queue size. Confirm everything with the Create Draft button in the ephemeral panel.
- **Participant pool**: `/draft join` and `/draft leave` let players opt in or out before the draft starts. Commissioners can force-add or force-remove with `/draft remove` and `/draft replace`.
- **Start and pick**: `/draft start` opens the draft. The on-clock participant runs `/draft pick <team>` — the pick claims the team directly to their account (same as `/team claim`), so there is nothing to sync afterward.
- **Live countdown**: timed drafts show a live Discord timestamp (`<t:deadline:R>`) so everyone can see how long the on-clock player has left.
- **Timeout actions**: when time runs out the bot autopicks, skips to the next participant, or pauses — based on the setting chosen at setup.
- **Commissioner controls**: `/draft skip` moves the clock to the next participant (with the configured skip rule), `/draft pause` freezes the clock, and `/draft resume` restarts it.
- **Personal queue**: each participant can pre-rank their preferred teams with `/draft queue add|remove|clear`. When autopick fires it picks the highest-ranked available team from the queue first, then falls back to the configured strategy.
- **Auto mode**: `/draft auto on` tells the bot to autopick for you immediately — useful if you know you will be away for your slot.
- **Ratings-based autopick**: the `Best by rating` strategy uses real CFB 27 launch ratings (all 138 FBS teams) when no personal queue is set, so autopicks go to the highest-rated available team rather than a random one.
- **Live draft board**: set a channel with `/admin channels draft_board` and Ball Boy keeps a single message there updated in real time — showing every pick made, who is currently on the clock, and what is coming up next.
