# v1.0.16 — Draft Mode — Channel Routing & Fixes

_Released 2026-07-07_

## What's New

**Cleaner draft channels.** You can now keep your draft board channel pristine and send the play-by-play somewhere else:

- **`/admin channels draft_updates:#channel`** — a new optional channel for the draft's live updates: on-the-clock notices (which **ping** the player whose turn it is), skips, and start/pause/resume/complete announcements.
- When `draft_updates` is set, the **draft board channel shows only the board** (the single live-updating message), staying free of noise.
- If you don't set a `draft_updates` channel, those updates fall back to the draft board channel (same as before) — so pings always reach players.
- **Picks** continue to post as normal **team-claim announcements** in your team-updates channel — a `/draft pick` is a team claim, so ownership, roles, and the roster board all update automatically.

## Fixes

- **Skips now render correctly on the board.** After a timed skip, the board no longer mislabels the wrong player as "skipped" — the player on the clock shows a live countdown, and the skipped player moves to their new spot as expected.
- **The pick countdown is live again.** The on-the-clock timer now displays and counts down properly (it was being masked by the skip bug above).
- **`/draft pick` is far more forgiving.** It now resolves your league even if the picker didn't tap the exact autocomplete suggestion, and finds the active draft even when the season field is left blank or entered loosely.
- **Board footer pick counter** now reflects the true draft position after skips.
