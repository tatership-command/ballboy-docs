# v1.0.32 — Schedule builder upgrades, team role colors, and streaming polish

_Released 2026-07-13_

## What's New
- **The in-app schedule builder is easier to work through.** Team cards now collapse — tap a team to expand its weeks, and use **Collapse all / Expand all** to keep things tidy. A progress pill on each team shows which ones still need games at a glance, and the **Apply** button is now pinned to the bottom so you never have to scroll to save your changes.
- **Custom team role colors.** Commissioners can set a per-league color for team roles with `/admin role_color`, with automatic brightening so darker colors stay readable on Discord.
- **Smarter `/stream`.** Going live now pings your configured stream role by default, works even when you don't pass a league, warns you when no stream role is set up, and pins the announcement so it's easy to find.

## Fixes
- **Cleaner roles.** Role sync now removes leftover team and conference roles for teams that are no longer owned.
