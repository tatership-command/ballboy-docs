# v1.0.30 — Role Sync No Longer Freezes the Bot

_Released 2026-07-11_

## Fixes

- **`/admin roles sync_all` no longer blocks every other command.** Previously a role sync held a bot-wide lock the entire time it worked, so while one league synced, commands in other leagues — even league autocomplete — stopped responding until it finished. Sync now does all its Discord role work without holding that lock, so other leagues keep working normally during a sync. The same fix was applied to conference-role setup on season creation.
- **`/admin roles sync_all` responds instantly.** It now acknowledges right away and posts the results when it finishes, instead of showing a spinner that could time out on large leagues.
