# v1.0.24 — Team Owners Get League Access

_Released 2026-07-08_

## Fixes

- **Owning a team now grants you access to your league's commands.** Previously, claiming a team didn't register you as a league member, so plain team owners could be denied commands like `/standings`, `/schedule`, and `/teams`. Now claiming, switching, or being assigned a team gives you member access automatically, and it's revoked when you leave or are released from all your teams.
- **Existing owners are unblocked automatically** — on this update the bot backfills member access for everyone who already owns a team, so no re-claiming is needed.
