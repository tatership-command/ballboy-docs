# v1.0.17 — Roster Board Game Profiles & Welcome Upgrades

_Released 2026-07-07_

## What's New

**Game profiles on the roster board** — every claimed team now shows the owner's linked game identity right under their name.

- Each roster-board row reads **team → Discord owner → 🎮 game profile** (e.g. `🎮 PSN: xX_TheDude_Xx`), pulled from the identity the owner selected in the Claim Activity.
- Owners who haven't linked a game identity show **🎮 game profile not set**, so commissioners can spot who still needs to connect their account at a glance.
- Works everywhere the board renders — the persistent roster board and the `/teams` command.

**A friendlier welcome message** — new members get everything they need to jump in, right in the welcome post.

- **Claim steps are now inline** — the welcome message spells out both ways to claim a team (`/team claim` and the apps & activities Claim Activity) instead of hiding them behind a button.
- **In-game league join info** — set your Madden/CFB in-game league name and join password with `/admin welcome`, and Ball Boy shows them in the welcome message so new members know exactly what to search for and type in-game. The password line reads "none required" when your league has no password, and the whole section is hidden until you configure it.
- The welcome title now greets members with your league's name.
