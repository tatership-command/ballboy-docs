# v1.0.4 — New-member welcome

_Released 2026-07-04_

## What's New
- New members get a friendly welcome with a private, click-to-view guide on how to claim a team (via `/team claim` or the Activity).
- Configurable triggers — on join, on a chosen role, or on gaining access to a chosen channel — via `/admin welcome`.
- Commissioners can post the how-to-claim guide on demand with `/welcome`.
