# v1.0.41 — Go Live in Voice, Public Schedule Announcements & a Game-Thread Fix

_Released 2026-07-23_

## What's New

**🔴 Go Live now points people to your voice channel**
When you start a Discord "Go Live" screen-share inside a voice channel and hit the 🔴 Go Live button on a game, Ball Boy's announcement now calls out the voice channel you're streaming in — and skips the platform link, since your stream is right there in Discord. Not screen-sharing in voice? Nothing changes: your stream link posts exactly as before.

**📅 Everyone sees when a game gets scheduled**
Setting a game's kickoff time now posts a public note in the game thread so both teams see it — not just the person who set it. The time auto-localizes to each viewer's timezone.

**🏈 Claim or connect your team without an active season**
In manual-mode leagues, `/team connect` and team claiming now work even when there's no active season — handy in the offseason or before your first season kicks off.

## Fixes

**🧵 Game threads no longer break when you rebuild a schedule**
If you rebuilt or re-imported a week's schedule (through the Scheduling Activity, or a bowl/CFP re-import), a not-yet-played game's thread could quietly lose its link to the game — its buttons would report "Could not find the game for this thread." Rebuilds now keep the thread connected. As part of the fix, the schedule editor also no longer mistakenly removes a game when two teams reference the same opponent in a single edit.
