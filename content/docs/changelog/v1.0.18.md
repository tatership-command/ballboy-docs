# v1.0.18 — Tidier Channels — Auto-Delete Activity Launch Cards

_Released 2026-07-07_

## What's New

**No more "used Launch" clutter** — when someone opens the Ball Boy Activity, Discord automatically drops a "used Launch" invitation card in the channel. Ball Boy now cleans those up for you.

- The launch card is deleted automatically as soon as it's posted, keeping your channels focused on the game.
- Prefer to keep them? Run `/admin launch_cards <league> keep` to leave the cards in place; `/admin launch_cards <league> suppress` turns auto-delete back on (the default).
- The setting is per-league, so each of your leagues can decide for itself.
